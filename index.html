AOV Calculator

Quickly and easily calculate your average order value (AOV). What is AOV? Average order value (AOV) is a key metric in ecommerce analytics that reflects the average amount spent each time a customer places an order over a defined period of time.

To calculate AOV, we divide the total revenue by number of orders taken:

Total Revenue ÷ Number of Orders = AOV.

To get started with the AOV Calculator, simply enter the start and end dates for the period you want to analyze. Then, input your total revenue and the total number of orders received during that period. Once you have filled in this information, press the "Calculate Your AOV" button to see your average order value (AOV).
